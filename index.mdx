# What is ERPsy?

ERPsy is an all-in-one business management platform designed for contractors, subcontractors, and service companies.

It combines CRM, project management, and accounting into a single system, allowing teams to manage the full lifecycle of their work — from initial lead to final payment.

## What ERPsy Helps You Do

- Track leads and customer relationships
- Create quotes and convert them into active contracts
- Manage jobs, work orders, and field operations
- Invoice customers and track payments
- Monitor financial performance

## Who ERPsy is For

ERPsy is built for:

- Service businesses managing jobs across multiple locations
- Contractors handling project-based work
- Teams that need both operational and financial visibility

ERPsy is designed to be powerful, while remaining simple enough for everyday use.
